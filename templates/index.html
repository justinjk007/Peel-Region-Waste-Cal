This page lets you download this year's waste collection schedule in your area as an i-Cal file if you are under the Peel region.

To use the service go to Peel regions website first get the "referrer" url, which can be obtained after typing your address and submitting it, I mean copy paste the damn thing from your browser tab after. Paste that url down here. Click and wait for the i-Cal file to be downloaded.
